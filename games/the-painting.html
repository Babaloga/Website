The Painting of Evie Ward

Played with a deck of cards and the game booklet.

(Available Here As A Tabletop Simulator Mod)

(Available Here As A .docx file)

Following the death of her father, Evie Ward has become trapped in a painting. She has 10 nights to escape by completing her late mother's magic paint set. If she fails she will be trapped forever.

Platform: Playing Cards

Duration: 1 month

Team Size: 3

Roles: Designer, Writer
